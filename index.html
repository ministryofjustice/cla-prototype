What is legal aid?

  • it can help you cover the cost of legal advice
  • it can get someone to speak for you in court
  • it can help you resolve family problems without going to court

You can only get legal aid if you are at immediate risk of serious harm, losing your home and have very limited money.

Use this service to see if you can get legal aid and find out where to go next for help. If you qualify for legal aid you will be contacted by our advisor within two working hours.

This service checks if you can get legal aid in England and Wales only.

All information you give us is confidential and will not be shared.
