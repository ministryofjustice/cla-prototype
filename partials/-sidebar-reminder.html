Who can get legal aid?

  • your income (or combined income if you have a partner) should not be more than £2,657 a month before tax
  • your savings (or combined savings if you have a partner) should not be more than £8,000
  • your income (or combined income if you have a partner) after certain outgoings have been deducted must not be more than £733 a month - this service will ask you more about these outgoings
