Can you get legal aid?

You'll only get legal aid if you can prove:

  • you and your partner have a combined income of less than £2,657 a month before tax
  • you and your partner have less than £8,000 in savings or in a home you own

Civil legal aid only covers problems to do with:

  • Housing
  • Debt
  • Discrimination
  • Family
  • Education

Civil legal aid does not cover problems to do with:

  • Employment
  • Immigration
  • Consumer issues
  • Criminal issues
