Can you get legal aid?

You'll only get legal aid if you can prove:

  • you have an income of less than £733 each month after your tax and costs such as housing, childcare and National Insurance contributions have been deducted (this includes your partner's income if you live together)
  • you have less than £8,000 each month in savings or in a home you own (this includes your partner's savings if you live together)

Civil legal aid only covers problems to do with:

  • housing
  • debt
  • discrimination
  • family
  • education

Civil legal aid does not cover problems to do with:

  • employment
  • immigration
  • consumer issues
  • criminal issues
