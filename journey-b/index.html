Do you qualify for legal aid?

Use this checker to see if you can get legal aid and find out where to go next for help.

Legal aid can help you cover the cost of legal advice, get someone to speak for you in court or help you resolve family problems.

You'll only get legal aid if you are at immediate risk of serious harm, losing your home or if you have very limited money.

Before you start

This checker is for non-criminal cases only. If you've been charged with a crime ask your solicitor about legal aid.

This checker assesses if you qualify for legal aid in England and Wales only.

All information you provide is confidential and will not be shared.
