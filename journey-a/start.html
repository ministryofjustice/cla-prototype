Before you start

By answering a series of questions you're about to check if you can get legal aid.

You must provide detailed information about your income, your savings, your property, any benefits you receive and also your partner's details.

You'll need your most recent pay-slips, bank statements and any benefit details to hand. Your result will depend on how serious your situation is and how much money you have.

If you do not qualify for legal aid, we'll offer you advice on where to get help.

This checker is for non-criminal cases only. If you've been charged with a crime ask your solicitor about legal aid.

This checker assesses if you qualify for legal aid in England and Wales only.

All information you provide is confidential and will not be shared.
