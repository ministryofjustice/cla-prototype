Before you start

You're about to check if you can get legal aid.

We'll take you through a series of questions that will provide us with information about whether you qualify for this service. Take time to answer the questions as fully and as accurately as possible.

If you do not qualify for legal aid, we'll offer you advice on where to get help.

This checker is for non-criminal cases only. If you've been charged with a crime ask your solicitor about legal aid.

This checker assesses if you qualify for legal aid in England and Wales only.

All information you provide is confidential and will not be shared.
