Your finances

You're about to check if you can get legal aid. You must provide detailed information about your income, your savings, your property, any benefits you receive and also your partner's details.

Before you start this section you'll need the following items:

  • your most recent pay-slips
  • your most recent bank statements
  • details of any benefits you receive

If you qualify for legal aid you will get to speak to an advisor. If you do not qualify we'll offer you suggestions on where to get help.

This checker is for non-criminal cases only. If you've been charged with a crime ask your solicitor about legal aid.

This checker assesses if you qualify for legal aid in England and Wales only.

All information you provide is confidential and will not be shared.
